Our Professional Scope

Cleanings Built for Modern Living

From brief touch-ups to intensive, multi-appliance refreshes, choose a service customized for your space's immediate needs.

Standard Home Cleaning

Regular maintenance cleaning to keep dust, grime, and debris away. Perfect for busy families on a weekly or bi-weekly cycle.

Deep Cleaning

A comprehensive top-to-bottom clean. Ideal for houses that have been neglected, or for your very first session with us.

Move-In / Move-Out

Ensure you secure your deposit or step into an immaculate fresh start. Fully covers all tracks, drawers, baseboards, and closets.

Recurring Cleaning

Establish automated, worry-free house maintenance with scheduling, dedicated cleaners, and customized checklists.

Kitchen Deep Clean

Meticulous focus on countertops, grease-prone vents, cabinetry, and full sanitization inside the oven and refrigerator.

Bathroom Sanitization

Intense details: grout scrub, tile clean-outs, lime descaling, glass screen shines, and comprehensive surface disinfecting.

Window Cleaning (Interior)

Streak-free interior windows, thoroughly cleared frames, wiped sills, and sparkling glass to flood your home with Arizona sun.

Post-Construction

Full removal of airborne sheetrock dust, fine paint residues, woodwork debris, and vacuum tracks left behind by remodelers.
